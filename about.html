My Background

I have a background in illustration, graphic design, photo editing, and of all things, metalsmithing. I spent four years learning how to make and repair jewelry before coming to UX — I enjoyed working with my hands, making and fixing beautiful objects that brought joy and held meaning for their owners.

I also have experience with front end coding - I started by coding my profile page on Neopets, and I've helped my mom with her home web design business since high school. I also coded this portfolio site from scratch.

Me and my partner, Calvin

Why UX?

My experience with visual design, web design, front end coding, and love of research make UX a natural fit. I was chaffing for a more challenging career, and between contract jobs took the opportunity to attend Prime Digital Academy's UX program to make an intentional career switch.

I was drawn to UX by a desire to help improve peoples’ lives through beauty, meaning, deliberate execution, thoughtful design, and elegance of function, on a larger and broader scale.
